# Competitor Comparison Tool
## See How You Stack Up Against Competitors

## What It Is

The Competitor Comparison Tool lets you compare your website's SEO performance against up to 3 competitors side-by-side. See exactly where you're falling behind and what opportunities exist to get ahead.

## Why We Built It

Competitive pressure is real. When you see your competitors outperforming you, it creates urgency to improve. This tool shows you exactly where the gaps are so you know what to fix first.

## How It Works

1. **Enter Your Website** - Your website URL
2. **Add Competitors** - Up to 3 competitor URLs
3. **Get Comparison** - Side-by-side SEO scores and analysis
4. **See Gaps** - Clear identification of where you're behind
5. **Get Recommendations** - Specific actions to close the gap

## What Gets Compared

- **Overall SEO Score** - Your score vs competitor average
- **Image Optimization** - Alt text coverage
- **Internal Linking** - Link structure comparison
- **Structured Data** - Schema markup presence
- **Mobile Optimization** - Responsive design elements
