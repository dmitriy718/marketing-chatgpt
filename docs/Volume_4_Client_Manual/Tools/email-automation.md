# Email Marketing Automation
## Automated Email Campaigns

## What It Is

An email marketing automation system that sends targeted, personalized email sequences to your subscribers based on their behavior, interests, and where they are in your customer journey.

## Why We Built It

Email marketing is one of the most effective marketing channels, but managing it manually is time-consuming. Automation lets you nurture leads and engage customers automatically, 24/7.

## How It Works

1. **Subscribe** - Users opt-in to your email list
2. **Tagging** - Subscribers are tagged based on interests/behavior
3. **Sequences** - Automated email sequences are triggered
4. **Personalization** - Emails are personalized to each subscriber
5. **Tracking** - Opens, clicks, and engagement are tracked
