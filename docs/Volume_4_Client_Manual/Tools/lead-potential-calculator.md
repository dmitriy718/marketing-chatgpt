# Lead Generation Potential Calculator
## Discover Your Revenue Potential

## What It Is

An interactive calculator that shows you exactly how much additional revenue you could generate each month by optimizing your marketing and conversion rates. See the money you're leaving on the table.

## Why We Built It

Many businesses don't realize their true revenue potential. This calculator shows you, in real numbers, what's possible with optimized marketing - creating urgency to take action.

## How It Works

1. **Enter Your Details**:
   - Industry type
   - Monthly website visitors
   - Current conversion rate (%)
   - Average deal value ($)
2. **Get Instant Results**:
   - Current monthly revenue
   - Potential monthly revenue
   - Additional revenue opportunity
   - Improvement percentage
3. **See Comparison**:
   - Your current rate vs industry average
   - Your potential optimized rate
   - Revenue gap visualization
