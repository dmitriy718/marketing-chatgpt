# Marketing Readiness Assessment
## Discover Your Marketing Maturity Level

## What It Is

An interactive 8-question quiz that assesses your marketing readiness across key areas: SEO, content, lead generation, analytics, social media, email marketing, conversion optimization, and brand presence.

## Why We Built It

Every business is at a different stage in their marketing journey. This assessment helps you understand where you are, where you need to go, and what to focus on next.

## How It Works

1. **Answer 8 Questions** - Quick, multiple-choice questions about your marketing
2. **Get Your Score** - Receive a percentage score (0-100%)
3. **See Your Level** - Classified as:
   - Getting Started (0-40%)
   - Beginner (40-60%)
   - Intermediate (60-80%)
   - Advanced (80-100%)
4. **Get Recommendations** - Specific areas to improve

## What Gets Assessed

### 1. SEO Optimization
How well is your website optimized for search engines?

### 2. Content Publishing
How often and consistently do you publish content?

### 3. Lead Generation
How sophisticated is your lead capture and nurturing system?

### 4. Analytics & Tracking
How well do you measure and analyze marketing performance?

### 5. Social Media Presence
How active and effective is your social media?

### 6. Email Marketing
How advanced is your email marketing automation?

### 7. Conversion Optimization
How optimized is your website for conversions?

### 8. Brand Presence
How strong is your brand recognition and presence?
