## SEO Auditor

### What does the SEO Auditor check?
The SEO Auditor analyzes:
- Title tags and meta descriptions
- Heading structure (H1, H2, etc.)
- Image alt text
- Internal and external links
- Structured data (JSON-LD)
- Mobile responsiveness
- Page load performance

### How often should I audit my website?
We recommend auditing your website:
- After major content updates
- Quarterly for ongoing monitoring
- Before launching new pages or sections

### Can I audit competitor websites?
Yes! You can audit any publicly accessible website URL.
