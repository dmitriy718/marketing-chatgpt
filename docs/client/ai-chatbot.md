# AI Chatbot Assistant
## 24/7 Instant Support

## What It Is

An AI-powered chatbot available 24/7 to answer your questions about Carolina Growth's services, pricing, and how we can help your business grow.

## Why We Built It

You have questions, and you want answers now - not during business hours. Our AI chatbot is always available to help, whether it's 2 AM or 2 PM.

## How It Works

1. **Click "Chat with us"** - Bottom right corner of any page
2. **Ask Your Question** - Type naturally, like talking to a person
3. **Get Instant Answer** - AI responds immediately
4. **Continue Conversation** - Ask follow-up questions
5. **Switch to Human** - Request human support anytime
