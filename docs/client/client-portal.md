# Client Portal
## Your Marketing Dashboard

## What It Is

A secure, private portal where you can access your account information, view active projects, download reports, and communicate with the Carolina Growth team.

## Why We Built It

You deserve transparency and easy access to your marketing information. The client portal puts everything in one place, available 24/7.

## How It Works

1. **Login** - Use your email and password
2. **View Dashboard** - See account status and overview
3. **Access Projects** - View active project status
4. **Download Reports** - Get your marketing reports
5. **Contact Support** - Message the team directly
