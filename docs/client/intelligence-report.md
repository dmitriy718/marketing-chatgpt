# Competitive Intelligence Report
## Comprehensive Website Analysis

## What It Is

A detailed analysis of any website (yours or a competitor's) that examines SEO performance, social media presence, contact accessibility, trust signals, and provides strategic recommendations.

## Why We Built It

Understanding your competitive landscape is crucial. This tool gives you deep insights into what competitors are doing right (or wrong) so you can make informed strategic decisions.

## How It Works

1. **Enter Website URL** - Any website you want to analyze
2. **Provide Your Email** - Required to receive the full report
3. **Get Comprehensive Analysis** - We examine:
   - SEO score and optimization
   - Social media presence and platforms
   - Contact information accessibility
   - Trust signals (testimonials, certifications, case studies)
4. **Receive Full Report** - Detailed PDF delivered to your email
