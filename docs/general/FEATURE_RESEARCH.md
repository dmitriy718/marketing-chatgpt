# Feature Research & Analysis
## Date: 2026-01-11

## Top 20 Features Identified

Based on web research and industry analysis of marketing agencies and AI marketing tools:

1. **AI Content Generator** - Automated blog posts, social media content, email campaigns
2. **Client Dashboard/Portal** - White-label reporting, analytics, project status
3. **Website SEO Auditor** - Free tool that grades websites and provides actionable insights
4. **Lead Scoring System** - AI-powered lead qualification and prioritization
5. **Social Media Scheduler** - Automated posting across platforms
6. **Email Marketing Automation** - Drip campaigns, segmentation, personalization
7. **Chatbot/AI Assistant** - 24/7 customer support and lead qualification
8. **Competitor Analysis Tool** - Monitor competitor websites, ads, SEO
9. **ROI Calculator** - Interactive tool showing marketing ROI (already have basic version)
10. **Case Study Generator** - Automated case study creation from client data
11. **Testimonial Collection System** - Automated request and display of client testimonials
12. **Landing Page Builder** - Drag-and-drop landing page creation
13. **A/B Testing Platform** - Built-in testing for campaigns and pages
14. **Marketing Calendar/Planner** - Visual calendar for campaigns and content
15. **Analytics Dashboard** - Unified view of all marketing metrics
16. **Keyword Research Tool** - SEO keyword suggestions and difficulty analysis
17. **Backlink Checker** - Analyze backlink profiles and opportunities
18. **Content Calendar** - Editorial calendar for content planning
19. **Client Onboarding Automation** - Streamlined client intake and setup
20. **Referral Program System** - Automated referral tracking and rewards

## Top 5 Features Selected

1. **AI Content Generator** (40 points) - High demand, high value, moderate difficulty
2. **Client Dashboard/Portal** (38 points) - High value, professional offering
3. **Website SEO Auditor** (36 points) - Excellent lead magnet, high demand
4. **Chatbot/AI Assistant** (37 points) - Modern, high demand, good for engagement
5. **Landing Page Builder** (38 points) - High value, but high difficulty (may need to simplify)
