9. **Free Resource Library** - Exclusive content for members
10. **Website Speed Test** - Instant value, actionable insights
11. **Content Performance Predictor** - AI-powered content scoring
12. **Lead Generation Potential Calculator** - Shows what they could achieve

14. **Free Strategy Session Booking** - Direct path to conversion

16. **Marketing Trends Dashboard** - Exclusive insights for members

20. **Marketing Maturity Assessment** - Comprehensive evaluation with roadmap

## Top 5 Features Selected (Eagerness to Join Focus)

1. **Competitor Comparison Tool** (50 points) - Creates FOMO, shows what they're missing
2. **Marketing Readiness Assessment** (46 points) - Personalized, engaging, shows value
3. **Competitive Intelligence Report** (48 points) - Exclusive value, creates urgency
4. **Lead Generation Potential Calculator** (45 points) - Shows future value, motivates action
5. **Marketing Maturity Assessment** (44 points) - Comprehensive, personalized roadmap

Alternative consideration: **Free Consultation Scheduler** (39 points) - Lower score but very direct path to conversion.

## Final Top 5 Selected

1. **Competitor Comparison Tool** - Highest urgency/desire score
2. **Marketing Readiness Assessment** - Personalized engagement
3. **Competitive Intelligence Report** - Exclusive value
4. **Lead Generation Potential Calculator** - Shows future value
5. **Free Consultation Scheduler** - Direct conversion path

These 5 features are designed to:
- Provide immediate, tangible value
- Create urgency and FOMO
- Show personalized value
- Make people feel they're missing out if they don't join
- Directly lead to sign-ups and consultations
